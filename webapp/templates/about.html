About FruitSight

Revolutionizing fruit quality assessment with artificial intelligence

Our Technology

FruitSight harnesses the power of advanced deep learning and computer vision to automatically grade fruits based on multiple quality attributes. Our proprietary AI model analyzes color patterns, size metrics, shape characteristics, ripeness indicators, and surface defects with remarkable accuracy, providing instant, objective quality assessments that rival human experts.

How It Works

Using our platform is simple and intuitive. Just upload a clear photo of your fruit, and our sophisticated AI system processes the image in seconds. The model examines hundreds of visual features, compares them against trained quality standards, and delivers a comprehensive analysis including an overall grade (A, B, or C) along with detailed confidence scores for each grade level.

Academic Project

This innovative project was developed by Okolo Uwaya Emmanuel as part of a Bachelor's degree in Computer Science at Lagos State University (LASU). It represents a culmination of extensive research in machine learning, computer vision, and web technologies, demonstrating the practical application of AI in solving real-world agricultural challenges.

Our Mission

We aim to democratize access to quality assessment technology, helping farmers, distributors, and consumers make informed decisions about fruit quality. By providing instant, objective grading, we reduce food waste, ensure fair pricing, and improve supply chain efficiency. Our vision is to expand this technology to support more fruits and vegetables, ultimately contributing to global food security and sustainable agriculture.
