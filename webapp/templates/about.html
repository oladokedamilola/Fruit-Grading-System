Project Overview

The Fruit Grading System is an AI-powered application that automatically assesses fruit quality using deep learning technology. It classifies fruits into three quality grades (A, B, C) based on visual characteristics such as color, size, shape, ripeness, and surface defects.

Grading Criteria

Grade A - Excellent

  • Uniform color typical for variety
  • No visible defects or blemishes
  • Perfect shape and size
  • Optimal ripeness

Grade B - Good

  • Slightly uneven color
  • Minor scratches or small spots
  • Slightly below average size
  • Acceptable ripeness

Grade C - Poor

  • Significant discoloration
  • Visible defects or bruises
  • Below acceptable size range
  • Over-ripe or under-ripe
